Tic Tac Toe Help


This application allows multiple users from all around the Globe to view a common webpage that is dynamically updated each time a user makes a change.
All components used in this application (board, X and O) are draggable and any changes made to them will be visible to every viewer of that world.
To begin, follow the instructions below:
1. Click on the Navigation button at the top right of your screen.
2. You can either create a New world or join any of the existing worlds by choosing the world name from the drop down list.
3. Click on the navigation icon to dismiss the menu.
4. Shake to reset the world to its original coordinates!!
Viewing the positions of all users: Navigate to the "Postions" tab to view the positions of all the users(current and past) of the world.
